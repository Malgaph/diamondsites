Packing Tips

To help give you the right knowledge and attitude when storing your belongings

01.

Safety First

Take extra effort to wrap fragile items separately. Place these things tightly in reinforced and strong boxes. Grab a sharpie and mark the box “FRAGILE” if it contains anything breakable. Be sure to label all your boxes for easy identification.

02.

Cover it up!

Dust will definitely creep into anything left in storage. Make sure that your boxes are filled to the top. Boxes that aren’t fill completely can shift during moving while the box corners and sides will probably cave in if there isn’t anything to support them. Foam Peanuts or Air packs are great for filling space in boxes.

03.

Balance is key

Each packed box should weigh roughly the same. Do not pack the box too heavy or you may not be able to lift it.

04.

Grab some friends!

Get some help from your friends by having one stack items once they are in the vehicle. A well packed truck will get you more value.

05.

Extra Cushion

Fragile items, such as mirrors or frames, should have extra padding with blankets or pillows.

06.

Strategic Packing

Spread out weight evenly in the truck by placing heaviest items against the back wall. Place similar sized boxes and items next each other.

07.

Plan Ahead

- Use protective sheeting or cardboard on the floor, and keep a small step ladder in the unit for higher to reach spots.
- Move larger items against the walls.
- Leave a walkway for easier access to your things.
- Get those valuable items away from the door, keep them out of view.

08.

Maximize Space

Try to use all available space in and around the stored furniture. Fill up any furniture item that’s hallow to get the most out of your available space. Look to fill in the following:

- Drawers
- Bookcases
- Washer and Dryer
